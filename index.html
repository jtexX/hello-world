I'm going to write a small article on why LED lights make your room the hang out (dorm)room

To LED or not To LED

To answer the question, definitely To LED, I mean why wouldn't you want to have nice lighting that you can manipulate to your heart's content. I mean really. However a lot of people make the common mistake of ordering ordinary LEDs without realizing one crucial difference... RGB. That's the magic word right there- R G B. RGB essentially means that the lights can change colors, and that matters. By being able to control the colors of the lights, you can harness the vibes. The best is buying 2 sets of RGB LEDs, and mixing different colors together to enhance said vibe. If you want to get really boujee you can buy the ones that change to music but who has that kind of money to spend.

So now you have the atmosphere, so go and SHOW IT OFF. Invite friends over! invite neighbors over, if you have the opportunity to show your homies, take it! Reel people into the, as the young folk say, immaculate vibes of what is soon going to be: The Legendary Hangout Room. The more time you have people spend in your room, the more time they have to appreciate the beauty of lighting that you have created. Granted you have nice decorations, good music, seating, and all that other stuff, your room will become the hangout room. Now if you don't want your room to be the hang out room but still want to enjoy luxury lighting, follow the simple technique of literally not inviting anyone over unless you actually want their company!. So at the end of the day, buy those LEDs, even if its for yourself because, the lighting do be going crazy.
